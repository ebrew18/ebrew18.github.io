Who am I?

I began learning to code in January of 2015, and it's become one of the best parts of my life. Seeing how websites and applications are designed and programmed is so interesting. When I first got interested in coding, it was the best decision of my life. I started learning from various websites, it made me incredibly happy to know that I didn't have to set foot in a classroom to pick up such a valuable skill.

I don't really have a favorite language, but I do enjoy Javascript and Ruby in particular. If I had to list any shortcomings, it would have to be that I am still inexperienced. But I am looking to change that, and I eagerly await any projects coming my way.
